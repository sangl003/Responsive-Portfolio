About me

I am Sharvari Sangle, Lead GIS Developer by profession. I am working in this industry for 6 years. I live in Maple grove and I am an Indian by origin. I love dancing , singing and I am an freelance abstract painter. I volunteer part at non-profit organizations and try my best to help and contribute to the community.

I am an outgoing and enthusiastic persona with attention to detail and hardworking qualities. I am a team player. I work as a Lead GIS developer at SRF consulting group. My role is to develop automation and analyze spatial data to create safer roadway systems.

I am responsible to provide GIS process improvement tools, developing automation using different programming platforms, creating web applications and web maps , cartographic PDF maps along with supporting GIS phases in the projects.25% of my job is dedicated to research and development and bring new technologies and initiatives in the company.

I have received Master’s degree in Geographic Information Science from the University of Minnesota and Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology from Pune University. I have a unique combination of IT and GIS, which allows me to offer GIS and programming skills. It allows me to bridge the technical gap between two areas.
